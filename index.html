About Us

Palace is a private day and boarding school, located in Dar-Es-Salaam, Kinondoni-Madale street, Tanzania. Palace offers rigorous academic programmed which aligns with the Tanzania National Exams council (NECTA) coupled with a range of extra curricular activities serving pupils from Pre-primary level through primary level. Our children experience the benefits of education that is academically challenging and personally rewarding.

Our strong reputation has developed through effective partnerships with the local community, strong academic results and a well-balanced curriculum which allows pupils to explore their passions, interests and talents.

We are extremely proud of our school and opportunities that exist for all pupils within Palace Schools. The pupils get to enjoy their classes more and ease the learning process as the class subjects are more engaging and draw out their interest in learning.

FERDINAND EMILY ILUNGUPalace Schools Head Teacher
